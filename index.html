Noumenal Research · the architecture of an AI scientist

A scientist that
shows its work.

Today an AI can announce a discovery and have no way to show it didn't invent it. We are building one that cannot keep a result without a machine-checked proof beside it, and that shows you exactly which results are still open.

Every result here is a goal, and beside it the witness: the evidence we offer for that goal, a proof, a figure, or an experiment we ran. Each is scored by four gates and left exactly as open as it really is. Not a catalog of answers. The process of making them, made visible.
